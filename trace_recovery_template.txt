# Firmware Trace Recovery Template

## Step 1: Capture Trace
$ trace_dump.sh --output trace_raw.log

## Step 2: Normalize Timestamps
$ sync_trace.py trace_raw.log > trace_synced.log

## Step 3: Filter for Errors and Warnings
$ grep -E 'ERROR|WARN' trace_synced.log > trace_filtered.log

## Step 4: Identify Drift Patterns
$ awk '{print $1, $2}' trace_filtered.log | uniq -c
